ABOUT US

We are a dance academy located in the Agora Gym. We are popular because we absolutely, positively love what we do. We live dance, breathe dance and we get to share dance with the world. We have earned the trust of our community, which is invaluable to us. In addition to having won multiple competitions across the country, we work relentlessly to be an organized, professional and responsible local adult and youth dance studio, while maintaining the core values of our world famous brand. Furthermore, different TV Channels and many companies have entrusted us to be the #1 provider of dance and choreography talent for movies, concerts, TV shows, commercials and award shows.

PRE-ACADEMY

We designed a pre-academy studying program to hone the dance skills of our members before they join the advanced class. Groups are divided by age :

Beginner class 3+ (20 per class)

Beginner class 6+ (20 per class)

Beginner class 9+ (30 per class)

Beginner class 14+ (30 per class)

ACADEMY

To provide the best possible dance education in a fun and welcoming environment through teaching excellence, fostering creativity, and nurturing respect for diversity. Focusing on technical expertise, strong technique, and discipline as well as improvisation, choreography, and body awareness. To guarantee that dancers become not only confident in their skills but also aware of the importance of teamwork, discipline, and respect for each other.
